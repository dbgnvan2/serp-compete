## [H2] The Anxiety Loop: Why "Stress Symptoms" Aren't the Whole Story
When relationship intensity rises, our first instinct is often to seek a clinical label. We ask:
- "Do I have an anxiety disorder?"
- "Is my partner a narcissist?"
- "How do I find a clinic for stress management?"

These questions represent an **Anxiety Loop**. Seeking a diagnostic label provides temporary relief, but it often reinforces chronic anxiety by isolating the individual from their relational context. Our approach intercepts this loop by shifting the focus from *what* you are feeling to *how* you are functioning together in the North Shore emotional system.
