By recognizing these patterns, "nightingale counselling" can help clients understand how they contribute to their current relational dynamics, aiding in the development of healthier interactions.

#### 4. Differentiation Strategy

A Bowen Family Systems approach is more effective for long-term functioning compared to individual 'tips and tools' because it addresses the root causes of relational anxiety. By focusing on differentiation of self, clients develop the capacity to navigate their relationships with more awareness and intentionality, reducing the need for reactive distancing or pursuing behaviors.

This approach empowers clients to:

- Foster healthier, more balanced relationships through understanding systemic patterns.

In conclusion, the Bowen Family Systems model, as applied in "nightingale counselling," offers a robust framework for understanding and transforming relational dynamics. By focusing on the system rather than the individual, clients can achieve deeper insights and more enduring change.
