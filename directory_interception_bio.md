# Directory Interception Bio: Living Systems Counselling

## Beyond Clinical Specialties: Focusing on the Relationship Process

Are you tired of being categorized by clinical labels? Most directory profiles list "Anxiety," "Depression," or "Attachment Issues" as isolated specialties. At Living Systems, we move beyond the medical model to observe the **Relationship Process**. 

We specialize in intercepting the common diagnostic traps that keep individuals and couples stuck. For example, if you feel chronically "anxious" in your partnership, you aren't just exhibiting a fixed attachment style; you are likely functioning as the **Pursuer** in a predictable emotional system. By reframing "Avoidant" behavior as **Reactive Distancing**, we help you see the reciprocity of your patterns.

Our approach is grounded in **Bowen Family Systems Theory**, focusing on **Differentiation of Self** rather than just providing symptom-fixing "tips." We help you move from "What is wrong with my partner?" to "How am I functioning in this system?" Whether you are navigating urban stress in Lonsdale or multigenerational patterns in Lynn Valley, we focus on the emotional system's facts.

By shifting the focus from individual pathology to the **Emotional Process**, we empower you to step out of the "Anxiety Loop" and into a more differentiated, sustainable way of relating.
